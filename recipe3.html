Buffet Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the rice according to the package instructions.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
  3. Add the mixed vegetables and cook until tender.
  4. Add the cooked rice to the skillet and stir to combine.
  5. Pour the soy sauce over the rice and vegetables, and stir well.
  6. Season with garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper.
  7. Cook for an additional 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy your buffet-style meal!
