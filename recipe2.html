Brunch Recipe

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of bread
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped vegetables (optional)

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs, milk, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  2. Heat a skillet over medium heat and melt the butter.
  3. Dip each slice of bread in the egg mixture, coating both sides.
  4. Place the bread slices in the skillet and cook until golden brown on both sides.
  5. Sprinkle cheese and vegetables on top of the bread slices.
  6. Cover the skillet and cook until the cheese melts.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious brunch!
